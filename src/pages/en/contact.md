## Join Us

We welcome prospective master’s and PhD students interested in soil organo-mineral interactions, tailing pedogenesis, mine-site ecological rehabilitation, rhizosphere and microbial processes, multi-scale characterization, and environmental data modeling.

The group also welcomes applications or inquiries from outstanding postdoctoral researchers with backgrounds in soil science, ecology, environmental science, geochemistry, microbiology, mineral/material sciences, data science, or related interdisciplinary fields.

We are open to collaborations with academic partners, industry, and restoration practitioners to connect mechanistic research on tailing pedogenesis and degraded soil rehabilitation with field-scale applications.
